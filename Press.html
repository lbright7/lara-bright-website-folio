Welcome!

It’s great to see you visiting my website!

I am currently a Year 12 VCE student studying at Assumption College, and have created this website to showcase my folio of work.


Throughout my course of schooling, I have managed to develop an extensive range of creative skills through completing a VET Certificate III in Creative and Digital Media.

My skills incorporate the proficient use of applications in the Adobe Creative Suite, Autodesk Maya and Epic Games’ Unreal Engine 4. In which, I have produced an extensive range of industry-standard work including 3D models, character design and environment design in addition to an animated trailer and significant web generating.

In my immediate future, I hope to further expand my expertise in these applications, and I plan on creating connections with like-minded individuals.

I also plan on further exploring the craft of acting.

O u t s t i n c t .

This website displays creative talents through an elaborate folio of work, primarily showcasing my game conceptualisation ‘Outstinct.’; a creative thought-provoking adventure/exploration game.

I would love to add that this concept was primarily inspired by Galactic Cafe’s 2011 game ‘The Stanley Parable’, as I wanted to create a storytelling game that I would enjoy; to be challenged upon preconceived beliefs, induce thoughtful actions to take place and incorporate the essence of mortality.

Ah, the sweet essence of mortality...
